Extend the tRPC context with lazy engine dependencies (LLMGateway, GatewayConfig, logger) and wire the `sendAnswer` mutation to call `InterviewFSM.submitAnswer()` synchronously instead of the current DB-only stub.

Output: Extended tRPC context, working sendAnswer mutation, unit tests for the wiring.
</objective>
